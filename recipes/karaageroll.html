Sumire Karaage Roll

What is a Sumire Karaage Roll?

The term Karaage exists in Japanese culinary, which means deep-frying poultry, other meat, and fish cooking techniques. The meat is marinated then coated with a seasoning mix.

Sumire Karaage roll is a light dish that can be eaten anywhere hence branding it as a snack. It is easy to make. However, it would help to eat it fresh. You can make Sumire Karaage Roll for your children and watch them light up with excitement, especially if they are anime fans too.
